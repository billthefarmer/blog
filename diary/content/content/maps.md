You may put [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org) maps in
diary pages with a geo URI `![osm](geo:<lat>,<lng>)`. Geo uris
received from other apps will be converted to
`![osm](geo:<lat>,<lng>)` syntax in the diary entry.
